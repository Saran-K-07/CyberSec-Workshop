# Foundations of Cybersecurity: Threats, Tools &amp; Practical Defence

Join an engaging online webinar on “Foundations of Cybersecurity: Threats, Tools & Practical Defence”, organized by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (Cyber Security) under the banner of the [Information Security Education and Awareness (ISEA) Project Phase–III](https://staysafeonline.in/) and the [Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE)](https://www.drngpit.ac.in/study/undergraduate/civil-engineering/prof-society/indian-socie-techn-educa). This session is designed to build strong fundamental understanding and practical skills for students.

This webinar introduces core cybersecurity concepts, real‑world threats, and hands‑on defensive practices across both system and human attack surfaces. Participants will gain clarity on terminology, attack lifecycle, and the role of secure configurations, monitoring, and user awareness in protecting modern digital environments.

## Platform and Participation

Mode: Online (Microsoft Teams).

E‑certificate will be provided to participants meeting the attendance requirements.

Prior basic computer knowledge is sufficient; no advanced prerequisites are required.
